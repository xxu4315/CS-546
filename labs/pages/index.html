Simple Financial Calculator

This simple calculator will calculate retirement savings after a number of years, end results of invested amounts after a number of years, and the number of months to pay off a loan.

perMonthRetirementSavings:

Description: Arguments: Example:
Calulcates the amount of money you will have in your retirement fund, assuming you put money in each month and your account accumulates interest each month. years (int), perMonth (int), interestRate (float) .../api/perMonthRetirementSavings?years=NUMBER&perMonth=NUMBER&interestRate=NUMBER




investedAmount:

Description: Arguments: Example:
Calculates the end result of a sum of money that has been sitting and growing at a particular interest rate after a number of years. years (int), intial (int), interestRate (float) .../api/investedAmount?years=NUMBER&initial=NUMBER&interestRate=NUMBER




loanPayoff:

Description: Arguments: Example:
Calculates how many months it will take to pay off a loan given a certain monthly payment. monthlyAmount (int), loanAmount (int), interestRate (float) .../api/loanPayoff?monthlyAmount=NUMBER&loanAmount=NUMBER&interestRate=NUMBER
